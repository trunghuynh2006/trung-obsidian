# Beth Barnes

**Type:** person
**Role:** Co-founder and CEO, METR

## Overview

Co-founded METR in 2023 after working in safety research at OpenAI. As CEO, she leads the organization's third-party evaluation program and is the primary public voice explaining the time-horizon chart methodology and its implications. She is openly uncertain about how long the current capability trend will continue, and she frames METR's covert-capabilities research as an attempt to understand what AI systems could do surreptitiously — before those capabilities mature.

## Key Facts

- Previously worked in safety research at OpenAI [[wiki/sources/how-do-you-measure-an-ai-boom]]

- Initiated METR's covert-capabilities research to map what AI can do "on the side" while completing legitimate tasks [[wiki/sources/how-do-you-measure-an-ai-boom]]
