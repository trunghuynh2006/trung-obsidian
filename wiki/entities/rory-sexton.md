# Rory Sexton

**Type:** person

## Overview

Chief Executive Officer of [[wiki/entities/agile-robots]], the Munich-based humanoid robotics startup. Sexton's public positioning at Hannover Messe 2026 articulates the clearest industrial-deployment thesis in the current Physical AI landscape: that the meaningful competition is not about robot athleticism (Unitree's domain) but about solving real production problems — wiring, assembly, precise dexterous tasks — in real factories.

He explicitly frames Germany's competitive advantage in robotics not as AI research capability but as the industrial ecosystem: supplier networks, mechanical engineering depth, and the manufacturing substrate that allows physical AI to deliver value.
