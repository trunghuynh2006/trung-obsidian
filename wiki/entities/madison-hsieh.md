# Madison Hsieh

**Type:** person
**Also known as:** None listed

## Overview

Madison Hsieh is a 25-year-old Amazon program manager used in the Guardian source as an example of AI-assisted prototyping. She used [[wiki/entities/cursor]] to create a social media app prototype in her spare time, compressing what would previously have required a longer timeline and a small engineering team.

## Key Facts

- Used Cursor to prototype a social media app in about a month. [[wiki/sources/gen-z-entrepreneurs-business-ai]]
- Remains employed at Amazon while developing the project as a possible future company. [[wiki/sources/gen-z-entrepreneurs-business-ai]]
- Frames entrepreneurship as a route to more impact than an early corporate role provides. [[wiki/sources/gen-z-entrepreneurs-business-ai]]
