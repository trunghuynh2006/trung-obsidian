# Bobby Woolf

**Type:** person
**Also known as:** —

## Overview

Bobby Woolf is a software engineer and architect who co-authored *Enterprise Integration Patterns* (2004, Addison-Wesley) with Gregor Hohpe. The book established the canonical vocabulary for message-based enterprise integration. Woolf was based at IBM at the time of writing.
