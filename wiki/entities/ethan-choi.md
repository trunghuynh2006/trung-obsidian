# Ethan Choi

**Type:** person
**Also known as:** None listed

## Overview

Ethan Choi is a partner at Khosla Ventures quoted in the Guardian source. He supplies the article's sharpest employer-side example of [[wiki/concepts/entry-level-job-erosion]]: venture partners and senior workers using AI to do work that associates previously handled.

## Key Facts

- Says expectations for entry-level workers have changed completely under AI. [[wiki/sources/gen-z-entrepreneurs-business-ai]]
- Describes moving from a sizeable associate team to "zero associates" because AI lets senior workers absorb the work. [[wiki/sources/gen-z-entrepreneurs-business-ai]]
- Argues that workers who get jobs in the new market will be those who build things. [[wiki/sources/gen-z-entrepreneurs-business-ai]]
