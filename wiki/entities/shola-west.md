# Shola West

**Type:** person
**Also known as:** None listed

## Overview

Shola West is a London-based brand consultant and content creator cited in the Guardian source. After layoffs and a weak market made conventional employment feel unreliable, she started her own consultancy and later partnered with brands including Paramount and Sony Music.

## Key Facts

- Was laid off in 2024 shortly after starting a new job. [[wiki/sources/gen-z-entrepreneurs-business-ai]]
- Started a brand consultancy because self-employment felt like the viable path in a weak market. [[wiki/sources/gen-z-entrepreneurs-business-ai]]
- Describes the new labor bargain as ownership replacing stability. [[wiki/sources/gen-z-entrepreneurs-business-ai]]

## Timeline
