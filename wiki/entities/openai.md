# OpenAI

**Type:** organization
**Category:** frontier AI research and product company

## Overview

Creator of the GPT model family, ChatGPT, and Codex. Within this wiki's current source set, OpenAI appears primarily as Anthropic's chief rival in the frontier model race and as one of the companies driving the rapid expansion of coding-capable and agentic AI systems.

## Relevance to This Wiki

OpenAI is central to two patterns already visible in this vault. First, Codex is one of the tools cited as triggering the November 2025 step change in AI coding capability. Second, OpenAI's frontier models are part of the broader cybersecurity shift in which the same systems can be used to find vulnerabilities, automate defensive review, or assist offensive hacking.

## Key Products (in current sources)

- **GPT** — general-purpose frontier language models; GPT-5.2 is one of the two models (alongside Claude Opus 4.5) cited as driving the METR time-horizon chart's acceleration to a 3–4 month doubling rate.
- **ChatGPT** — consumer and professional interface for GPT models.
- **Codex** — coding-focused system cited as part of the 2025 coding-agent inflection.
