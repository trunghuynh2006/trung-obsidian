# Stephen Dubner

**Type:** person
**Also known as:** Stephen J. Dubner

## Overview

Stephen J. Dubner is a journalist and author, formerly of *The New York Times*, who co-wrote *Freakonomics* with economist Steven Levitt. His role in the collaboration was translating Levitt's econometric arguments into narrative — finding the story inside the data. The Freakonomics project has expanded into sequels, a podcast, and a broader media brand.

## Key Facts

- NYT journalist; co-author of *Freakonomics* (2005, expanded 2006) with Steven Levitt [[wiki/sources/freakonomics-introduction]]
- Brought the book's vignette structure and accessible voice to Levitt's research
