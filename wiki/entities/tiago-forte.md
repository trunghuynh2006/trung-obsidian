# Tiago Forte

**Type:** person
**Role:** Author, productivity educator, founder of Forte Labs

## Overview

Author of *Building a Second Brain* (2022), the book that popularized the PARA/CODE framework for personal knowledge and project management. Forte Labs runs courses on productivity and knowledge management.

Forte's work builds on David Allen's *Getting Things Done* tradition, extending it to handle modern information overload and content-heavy knowledge work. His central contribution is PARA — a four-container storage system ordered by action relevance — and progressive summarization as a lightweight distillation method.

## Key Works

- *Building a Second Brain* (2022) — PARA system, CODE workflow, progressive summarization.
- Forte Labs courses — online courses on PKM and productivity.

## Key Ideas

- The "second brain" concept: externalize cognitive overhead to a trusted system to achieve "mind like water."
- PARA as a production system, not a filing system: nothing has a permanent home; information moves toward where it's actionable.
- Completed projects as "the blood flow of your second brain."
