# Friedrich Merz

**Type:** person

## Overview

Chancellor of Germany as of 2026. Merz has become a visible political figure in the German industrial AI narrative, both through first-hand exposure to Chinese robotics advances and through active endorsement of AI adoption in German manufacturing. His February 2026 China visit — where he witnessed Unitree robots performing kung fu and boxing — became a widely cited illustration of China's robotics lead.

At Hannover Messe 2026, Merz visited the Agile Robots stand and delivered a speech calling for AI to be "embedded" in German industrial sectors, specifically targeting the small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs) that form the backbone of the German economy.
