# Gallup

**Type:** org
**Also known as:** Gallup Organization, Gallup Inc.

## Overview

Gallup is a US-based analytics and advisory firm best known for public opinion polling, workplace engagement research, and large-scale social surveys. In the context of this wiki, Gallup is the primary research organization behind the 2026 Gen Z AI attitudes survey conducted in partnership with the Walton Family Foundation and GSV Ventures. The named researcher on that study is Zach Hrynowski, a senior education researcher at Gallup.

## Key Facts

- Conducted the February–March 2026 survey of 1,500+ Americans ages 14–29 on AI attitudes [[wiki/sources/gen-z-ai-gallup-study]]
- Partners on the Gen Z survey: Walton Family Foundation and GSV Ventures (an edtech-focused VC firm)
- Zach Hrynowski (senior education researcher) is the named analyst; expressed surprise at the sharpness of the year-over-year sentiment shift
