# Mark Cuban

**Type:** person
**Also known as:** None listed

## Overview

Mark Cuban is an entrepreneur, investor, minority owner of the Dallas Mavericks, former *Shark Tank* investor, and cofounder of Cost Plus Drugs. In this wiki's current source set, he appears as a public business figure arguing that AI disruption can create opportunities for young workers who learn how to implement practical agent workflows for companies.

His contribution is less technical than strategic: he identifies a labor-market opening between frontier AI capability and ordinary business adoption. Companies may know AI matters but still lack the operational skill to turn it into reliable agents for customer service, scheduling, invoicing, and other unglamorous workflows.
