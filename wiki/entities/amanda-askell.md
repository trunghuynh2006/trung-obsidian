# Amanda Askell

**Type:** person
**Role:** House philosopher at Anthropic

## Overview

Philosopher employed at Anthropic to design and articulate the foundational character and values of Claude. Described in the source as responsible for giving Claude "a foundational guide to its character full of references to Aristotelian concepts like practical wisdom (*phronesis*)."

Her work represents a direct application of virtue ethics to AI alignment — the bet that building consistent, character-grounded values into a model is more robust than rule-based safety measures.

## Significance

Askell's work at Anthropic is philosophically notable: it's a live experiment testing whether virtue ethics can serve as an alignment framework at frontier AI scale. The [[wiki/concepts/emergent-misalignment]] finding supports the underlying hypothesis: character in LLMs appears to be unified, which means building genuinely good character may be more robust than patching specific harmful behaviors.
