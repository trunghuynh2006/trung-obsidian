# Agile Robots

**Type:** org
**Also known as:** Agile Robots AG

## Overview

Munich-based humanoid robotics startup founded by Chinese entrepreneur Zhaopeng Chen. Agile Robots develops humanoid robots with a stated focus on value-added industrial tasks — precise dexterous work such as electronic wiring in cars and phone assembly — rather than the performance demonstrations that have made Chinese competitors like Unitree internationally visible.

The company positions itself at the intersection of European industrial expertise and AI-driven robotics: CEO Rory Sexton emphasizes Germany's "ecosystem of suppliers" and "very strong expertise in mechanical engineering and automation" as the competitive substrate Agile Robots draws on, rather than competing head-to-head with Chinese manufacturers on robot athleticism.
