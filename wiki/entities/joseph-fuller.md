# Joseph Fuller

**Type:** person
**Also known as:** None listed

## Overview

Joseph Fuller is a professor at Harvard Business School and co-director of the Project on Workforce at Harvard. In the Guardian source, he provides the clearest analytical framing for Gen Z's AI-era career problem: if AI erases the lower rungs of the ladder, young workers need another way to reach higher-skill work.

## Key Facts

- Identifies recent graduates as especially exposed because entry-level jobs often contain routine cognitive work. [[wiki/sources/gen-z-entrepreneurs-business-ai]]
- Frames entrepreneurship as one way for young workers to "make your own ladder." [[wiki/sources/gen-z-entrepreneurs-business-ai]]
- Argues that opportunities will still exist, but not necessarily in the forms conventional career advice prepared students for. [[wiki/sources/gen-z-entrepreneurs-business-ai]]
