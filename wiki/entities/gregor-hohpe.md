# Gregor Hohpe

**Type:** person
**Also known as:** —

## Overview

Gregor Hohpe is a software architect best known for co-authoring *Enterprise Integration Patterns* (2004, Addison-Wesley) with Bobby Woolf. The book codified the vocabulary of message-based integration that became the lingua franca of enterprise middleware design. He has also worked at Google (on cloud transformation and architecture) and the SEI/ISRM.

## Key Facts

- Co-authored *Enterprise Integration Patterns* (2004) — the canonical catalog of messaging patterns [[wiki/sources/enterprise-integration-patterns-ch1]]
- Introduced or popularized patterns such as Message Broker, Content-Based Router, Aggregator, and Process Manager [[wiki/sources/enterprise-integration-patterns-ch1]]
