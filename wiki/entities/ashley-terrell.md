# Ashley Terrell

**Type:** person
**Also known as:** None listed

## Overview

Ashley Terrell is a University of Hawaii graduate used in the Guardian source as the lead example of Gen Z adapting to a blocked entry-level job market. After failing to find the marketing role she expected and working retail at Home Depot, she began creating brand videos directly for companies, building a portfolio that led to paid content work and a part-time marketing role.

## Key Facts

- Graduated from the University of Hawaii in 2024 with a business administration degree and student marketing experience. [[wiki/sources/gen-z-entrepreneurs-business-ai]]
- Built a branded-content portfolio after conventional marketing applications failed. [[wiki/sources/gen-z-entrepreneurs-business-ai]]
- Sold a video to Jamba Juice for use as an Instagram and TikTok ad. [[wiki/sources/gen-z-entrepreneurs-business-ai]]
