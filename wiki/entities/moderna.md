# Moderna

**Type:** org

## Overview

Moderna is a pharmaceutical company known for mRNA vaccine development. In the context of this wiki, it appears as a structural organizational design example: Moderna merged its HR and IT functions under a single executive, appointing the CHRO as Chief People and Digital Technology Officer (CPDT). This is cited as one of the most concrete responses to the AI governance vacuum — eliminating the cross-functional alignment problem by eliminating the boundary between the two functions.

## Key Facts

- Moderna merged its CHRO and CIO roles into a single Chief People and Digital Technology Officer [[wiki/sources/whats-ais-real-failure-no-ones-actually-in-charge]]
- The executive appointed to the merged role has expertise in both digital transformation and HR — a dual-domain background that makes the combination functional rather than ceremonial [[wiki/sources/whats-ais-real-failure-no-ones-actually-in-charge]]
- The article labels this model "an extreme example" even among AI-forward companies
