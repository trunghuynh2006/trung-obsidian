# Sergey Levine

**Type:** person

## Overview

Sergey Levine is a UC Berkeley professor focused on AI for robotics and a co-founder of Physical Intelligence. He is one of the leading academic researchers in learning-based robot control and a prominent voice on the question of whether generalist foundation models can replace task-specific robot programming.

He is notable for drawing an explicit analogy between current Physical AI inflection points and early GPT-2 — the moment language models began producing outputs their training data did not obviously predict.
