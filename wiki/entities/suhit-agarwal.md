# Suhit Agarwal

**Type:** person
**Also known as:** None listed

## Overview

Suhit Agarwal is a University of Southern California graduate cited in the Guardian source as an example of AI-enabled early-career reinvention. After failing to get interviews at Google and other Big Tech companies, he moved into startup work and founding-engineer roles, using AI tools including Claude Code to take on responsibilities larger than he expected at his career stage.

## Key Facts

- Graduated from USC in 2025 with a computational and applied mathematics background. [[wiki/sources/gen-z-entrepreneurs-business-ai]]
- Applied repeatedly to Google for internships and jobs without getting an interview. [[wiki/sources/gen-z-entrepreneurs-business-ai]]
- Used AI tools including Claude Code in founding-engineer roles. [[wiki/sources/gen-z-entrepreneurs-business-ai]]
- One startup he helped found was acquired, producing a small equity payout. [[wiki/sources/gen-z-entrepreneurs-business-ai]]
