# Scott Young

**Type:** person
**Also known as:** none

## Overview

Scott Young appears in this wiki as the author associated with **Ultralearning**, a framework for intense, self-directed skill acquisition. In the current source set, his importance is less biographical than conceptual: he is the named source behind a system that combines metalearning, focus, directness, drill, retrieval, feedback, retention, intuition, and experimentation.

## Key Facts

- Presented as the author of *Ultralearning*, a book on accelerating self-improvement and skill acquisition [[wiki/sources/ultralearning]]
- Associated with an approach centered on self-direction, intensity, and practical learning by doing [[wiki/sources/ultralearning]]
- The framework summarized here is built around nine principles: metalearning, focus, directness, drill, retrieval, feedback, retention, intuition, and experimentation [[wiki/sources/ultralearning]]
