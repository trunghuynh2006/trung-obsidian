# Acronis

**Type:** organization
**Also known as:** none

## Overview

Acronis appears in this wiki as the backup vendor the source uses to illustrate a recovery strategy that goes beyond Microsoft 365's native protections. Its role in the current source is not a neutral market survey but a product-positioning example: backup, ransomware-aware recovery, and immutable storage are presented together as the differentiators that matter when a robotics firm needs to restore clean project context quickly.

Because the article is promotional in tone, the Acronis claims should be treated as directional rather than independently validated. Still, the example is useful because it names the features the author believes matter most in practice: isolated storage, clean restores, and the ability to recover after compromise rather than only after accidental deletion.
