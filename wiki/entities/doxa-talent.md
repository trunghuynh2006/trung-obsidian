# DOXA Talent

**Type:** org
**Also known as:** DOXA Talent

## Overview

DOXA Talent appears in the wiki as the author's operating example for early, imperfect AI adoption. In the current source set, its importance is not corporate biography but managerial practice: it is presented as a distributed organization that tried to make AI learning structured, social, and visible rather than informal and isolated.

The article portrays DOXA Talent as managing more than 800 team members across six countries without a physical office. Its AI approach emphasized committee-based shared ownership, governance guardrails, training, outside partnership, and peer learning. Because all of this comes from a first-person opinion piece, the organization should be treated as an illustrative case study, not a neutral evidence source.
