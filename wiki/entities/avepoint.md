# AvePoint

**Type:** organization
**Also known as:** none

## Overview

AvePoint appears in this wiki as the source's example of a SaaS-style Microsoft 365 backup platform. The article highlights automation, search, and bulk restore as the relevant differentiators, especially when a team needs to clean up after user error or a wider incident affecting many items.

Like the other vendor mentions in this source, the treatment is brief and promotional. Its real value to the wiki is to mark one axis of the backup-market comparison: ease of operation and large-scale restore ergonomics.
