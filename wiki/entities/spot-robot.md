# Spot

**Type:** product
**Also known as:** Spot robot, Boston Dynamics Spot

## Overview

Spot is a quadruped (four-legged) mobile robot built by Boston Dynamics, designed for autonomous inspection in environments that are too hazardous, inaccessible, or repetitive for human workers. It moves across uneven terrain and through industrial facilities, capturing visual data for analysis.

Spot's commercial value is delivered through Boston Dynamics' Orbit software platform, which hosts the AI Visual Inspection (AIVI) and AIVI-Learning systems. As of 2026, these systems have been upgraded with Google DeepMind's Gemini Robotics-ER 1.6, shifting Spot's inspection capability from object detection to full spatial reasoning — including instrument reading and success detection.
