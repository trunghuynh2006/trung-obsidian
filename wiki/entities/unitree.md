# Unitree

**Type:** org
**Also known as:** Unitree Robotics

## Overview

Chinese humanoid robotics manufacturer known for highly capable and visually striking robot demonstrations. Unitree's robots performing kung fu and boxing were seen by German Chancellor Friedrich Merz during his February 2026 China visit — a moment that became a political catalyst for German industrial AI investment. Unitree has been a recurring presence at Hannover Messe, Germany's largest industrial trade fair.

Unitree represents the "demo capability" pole in the 2026 physical AI landscape: robots that are athletically impressive but not yet positioned primarily around specific industrial value-added tasks. European competitors like Agile Robots explicitly use Unitree as the benchmark to match technically, while differentiating on industrial application focus.
