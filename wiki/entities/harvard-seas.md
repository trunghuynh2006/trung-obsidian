# Harvard SEAS

**Type:** org
**Also known as:** Harvard John A. Paulson School of Engineering and Applied Sciences, SEAS

## Overview

Harvard SEAS (John A. Paulson School of Engineering and Applied Sciences) is the engineering and applied science school at Harvard University, affiliated with both Harvard's Faculty of Arts and Sciences (FAS). It hosts interdisciplinary research spanning applied mathematics, computer science, electrical engineering, mechanical engineering, and bioengineering.

In the context of this wiki, Harvard SEAS is notable as the institutional home of [[wiki/entities/l-mahadevan]]'s lab, which produced the RAnts swarm robotics research — one of the leading examples of bio-inspired, decentralized physical AI systems.

## Key Facts

- Home of L. Mahadevan's lab studying social insect-inspired robotics and physical phenomena in biological systems [[wiki/sources/simple-robots-ants-excavate]]
- Jointly affiliated with the Faculty of Arts and Sciences (FAS) for cross-disciplinary work [[wiki/sources/simple-robots-ants-excavate]]
