# Physical Intelligence

**Type:** org
**Also known as:** pi.ai, PI

## Overview

Physical Intelligence is a San Francisco-based robotics AI startup founded in 2024. It has quietly become one of the most closely watched AI companies in the Bay Area, focused on building general-purpose foundation models for robot control. Its research strategy mirrors what happened with LLMs: instead of training specialist models per task, it trains a single generalist model that can be coached through unfamiliar tasks in plain language.

The company is notable for refusing to offer investors a commercialization timeline while still attracting serious institutional capital — a posture that signals long-horizon research conviction rather than near-term product focus.
