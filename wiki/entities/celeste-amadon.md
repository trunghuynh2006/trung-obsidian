# Celeste Amadon

**Type:** person
**Also known as:** None listed

## Overview

Celeste Amadon is a 22-year-old founder cited in the Guardian source. She turned down an investment banking internship at JP Morgan to start Known, an AI-supported dating app company, and later raised more than $9 million in venture funding.

Her case is the article's strongest example of a direct jump from internship-level experience to CEO responsibility. It also highlights the skill gap this creates: young founders must learn hiring, firing, management, and capital allocation by doing.

## Key Facts

- Turned down a JP Morgan investment banking internship to start Known. [[wiki/sources/gen-z-entrepreneurs-business-ai]]
