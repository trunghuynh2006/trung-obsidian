# Known

**Type:** product / company
**Also known as:** None listed

## Overview

Known is an AI-supported dating app company founded by [[wiki/entities/celeste-amadon]]. In the Guardian source, it appears as a case study of Gen Z entrepreneurship: a young founder leaving the expected finance path to build a company and learn executive skills in real time.

## Key Facts

- Founded by Celeste Amadon after she turned down an investment banking internship. [[wiki/sources/gen-z-entrepreneurs-business-ai]]
- Uses AI to help singles meet. [[wiki/sources/gen-z-entrepreneurs-business-ai]]
- Raised more than $9 million in venture capital, according to the Guardian source. [[wiki/sources/gen-z-entrepreneurs-business-ai]]
