# Chris Painter

**Type:** person
**Role:** President, METR

## Overview

President of METR. His most quoted statement in the current source: "This is the first year where it feels like it might be automated this year" — referring to full automation of AI research and development. He identifies that pathway (AI automating its own R&D cycle) as the most plausible route to an intelligence explosion, and considers it close enough to merit explicit concern rather than dismissal.

## Key Facts

- Believes the most likely pathway to an intelligence explosion runs through full automation of AI R&D [[wiki/sources/how-do-you-measure-an-ai-boom]]
- 2026 is, in his view, the first year when that possibility feels calendrically near rather than abstractly distant [[wiki/sources/how-do-you-measure-an-ai-boom]]
