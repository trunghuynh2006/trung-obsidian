# AI Officer Institute

**Type:** org
**Also known as:** none listed

## Overview

AI Officer Institute appears in the wiki as an outside partner cited in the DOXA Talent case study. In the current source set, it represents the pattern of bringing in external support to accelerate AI learning and confidence-building without pretending the organization already has all necessary internal capability.

The source does not provide much detail about the institute itself. Its relevance here is practical: it is presented as part of a broader AI adoption approach that combined outside partnership with internal committees, governance, training, and peer learning.

## Key Facts

- The article says DOXA Talent partnered with the AI Officer Institute to go further and faster in its AI adoption efforts [[wiki/sources/why-waiting-to-adopt-ai-is-riskier-than-you-think]]
