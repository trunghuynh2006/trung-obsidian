# Hannover Messe

**Type:** event

## Overview

The world's largest industrial technology trade fair, held annually in Hannover, Germany. With 3,000+ exhibitors in 2026, Hannover Messe is the primary global stage for industrial technology announcements, product demonstrations, and signals of strategic direction in manufacturing, automation, and industrial AI.

In 2026, physical AI — robots and AI systems applied to hands-on, real-world industrial tasks — was the dominant theme, with German, European, and Chinese manufacturers presenting humanoid and collaborative robots alongside industrial AI platforms.
