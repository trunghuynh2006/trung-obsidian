# Ajeya Cotra

**Type:** person
**Role:** AI safety researcher, METR (recently joined)

## Overview

Longtime AI safety researcher who recently joined METR. Previously known for probabilistic work on when transformative AI might arrive. Her framing of METR's mission: "an organization that asks questions selected for what we think would be most valuable for the world to know about A.I. and its risks — and then the answers are what they are." This neutral-instrument framing is deliberate: METR does not root for any particular outcome; it tries to find out what is actually happening.
