# KPMG

**Type:** organization
**Also known as:** none

## Overview

KPMG appears in this wiki as a management and advisory institution producing research-backed arguments about how organizations should respond to AI-era volatility. In the current source set, its role is not technical AI development but strategic interpretation: it argues that firms should continue transformation during uncertainty rather than waiting for a return to stable conditions.

## Key Facts

- The article attributes research to KPMG on how 1,800-plus U.S. public companies performed during pandemic-era uncertainty [[wiki/sources/this-is-the-biggest-risk-a-company-can-take-during-the-age-of-ai]]
- KPMG's framing is that firms accelerating transformation during turbulence outperformed cautious peers on shareholder return and revenue growth [[wiki/sources/this-is-the-biggest-risk-a-company-can-take-during-the-age-of-ai]]
- The practical recommendations emphasized are outcome-based funding, reduced consensus drag, AI-native workflow redesign, and a bias for action [[wiki/sources/this-is-the-biggest-risk-a-company-can-take-during-the-age-of-ai]]
