# DFKI

**Type:** org
**Also known as:** German Research Centre for Artificial Intelligence; Deutsches Forschungszentrum für Künstliche Intelligenz

## Overview

Germany's national AI research centre. In the context of industrial Physical AI, DFKI represents the institutional bridge between Germany's deep manufacturing base and AI capability development. DFKI head Antonio Krueger made a notable claim at Hannover Messe 2026: Germany's factory-floor industrial data is of higher quality than that of both the United States and China — an underappreciated structural advantage in the race to train capable physical AI systems.

The acknowledged limitation: this data advantage is currently fragmented and siloed across individual firms, with no national strategy to aggregate it for AI training at scale.
